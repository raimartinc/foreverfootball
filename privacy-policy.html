Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2025

Welcome to foreverfootball.online, a fan-driven archive celebrating football’s legacy with over 1000 teams across FIFA’s 211 nations. We’re committed to protecting your privacy while delivering team stats, logos, and stories.

Information We Collect

We don’t collect personal data directly—no sign-ups or profiles required. Our content is built from public football data (team names, logos, trophies). However, we use Google Analytics (ID: G-3MZ66WWDR4) to track anonymized info like page views, device type, IP address, and approximate location—no names or emails involved unless you provide them. Google AdSense (ID: ca-pub-9925950012374716) may also collect data to serve ads. If you use our Contact page, we collect optional info (name, email) and required messages via Formspree, only if you choose to send us feedback.

How We Use Information

Analytics data improves your experience—better navigation, richer team details, and a smoother map. AdSense data supports relevant ads to keep the site running. Contact form data (name, email, message) is used solely to respond to your inquiries or suggestions. We don’t sell, trade, or share any of this with anyone outside these services.

Cookies and Tracking

Google Analytics and AdSense use cookies to track site usage (e.g., visits, ad clicks). These are anonymized and don’t identify you personally unless you submit identifiable info via our Contact page. You can opt out by disabling cookies in your browser—no impact on your visit.

Third-Party Services

We host on Netlify (see their Privacy Policy). Google Analytics and AdSense are powered by Google (check their Privacy Policy). Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree (see their Privacy Policy). Team logos come from TheSportsDB.com, and some summaries pull from Wikipedia—both public, non-personal sources.

Security

We use a strict Content Security Policy (CSP) to protect your browsing—only trusted scripts and resources load here. Contact form data is securely handled by Formspree’s servers.

Your Rights

Analytics and AdSense data is anonymized, so there’s nothing personal to delete. If you submit info via our Contact page, you can request its removal by emailing us at email@. We’ll delete it promptly unless required by law to retain it.

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or suggestions, visit our Contact page or email email@. Form submissions (name, email, message) are optional and only used to reply to you.

Changes to This Policy
