Forever Football Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2025

Welcome to foreverfootball.online, where football’s legacy thrives with over 900 teams and counting. We’re committed to keeping your privacy intact while delivering the ultimate football experience.

Information We Collect

We don’t collect personal data directly—no sign-ups or profiles needed. Our site runs on public football stats (team names, trophies, logos) to fuel your fandom. However, we may use third-party tools like Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which collect anonymized data such as page views, device type, and location—no names or emails attached.

How We Use Information

Any data we gather is for improving your experience—think better navigation, richer team details, and smarter maps. We don’t sell, trade, or share it with anyone, period. Analytics help us see what’s clicking; ads (via AdSense) keep the lights on.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies through Google AdSense and Analytics to enhance functionality and serve relevant ads. These track things like site visits or ad clicks, but nothing identifiable to you. Prefer to opt out? Adjust your browser settings to block cookies—no penalties here.

Third-Party Services

Our site’s hosted on Netlify—check their Privacy Policy. Google AdSense serves ads; see Google’s Privacy Policy for how they handle data. Wikipedia powers some team summaries—public data, no personal info involved.

Your Rights

Since we don’t collect personal data, there’s nothing to delete or request. Questions? Hit us up below—we’re all ears.

Contact Us

Got privacy concerns or ideas? Drop a message—no email required unless you want a reply.




Changes to This Policy
